About Me

Brady is a lifelong learner with a passion for computer programming and writing. He currently attends a web development bootcamp at the University of Minnesota, which he intends to use to jump-start a career in the programming field.


He grew up in Minnesota and accordingly spends a disproportionate amount of time inside, hiding from the cold weather. Come spring, however, it's hard to find him inside.


He is currently working on prototyping a social media website with a partner that he hopes will provide a unique new experience for connecting on the web.
